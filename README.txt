Black Swan Stock Simulator

Black swan stock simulator is an online social stock market game.

Compete against friends as you build your fortune, buying and selling stocks in a dynamic economy.
Earn rewards and unlock new ways to earn money as you progress.
Spend your hard earned cash on items to improve your reputation, giving you access to more information.
